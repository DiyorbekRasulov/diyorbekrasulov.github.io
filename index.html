Thread-Safe Producer–Consumer Queue

C • POSIX Threads

Designed a queue that safely supports multiple producer and consumer threads without data races or deadlocks.

What I did

Used mutexes and condition variables to synchronize access to shared data while avoiding busy waiting and ensuring correctness under concurrency.

What I learned

  • Why condition variables must be used in loops
  • How small synchronization mistakes create subtle bugs
  • How to reason about thread interleavings
